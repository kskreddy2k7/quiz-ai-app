# Privacy Policy for S Quiz - AI Learning Platform

**Last Updated:** February 8, 2026

## Introduction

S Quiz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Use Google Sign-In authentication
- Contact us for support

This may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Google profile information (when using Google Sign-In)
- Profile photo (if provided via Google)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
- Quiz performance data (scores, topics studied)
- Language preferences
- Technical information (IP address, browser type)

### Content You Provide
- Topics you enter for quiz generation
- Files you upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
- Questions you ask the AI tutor
- Quiz responses and answers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the Service**
   - Generate personalized quizzes
   - Provide AI tutoring responses
   - Process uploaded files
   - Remember your preferences

2. **Improve the Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features
   - Optimize AI performance

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send service-related notifications
   - Respond to your inquiries
   - Provide customer support

4. **Security and Safety**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Protect user safety

## Data Storage and Security

### File Processing
- **Uploaded Files:** Files you upload are processed temporarily and are NOT stored permanently on our servers
- **Processing Time:** Files are deleted immediately after quiz generation
- **Security:** All file transfers use encrypted connections (HTTPS)

### Account Data
- Account information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Google Sign-In uses OAuth 2.0 secure authentication

### Quiz Data
- Quiz questions and your responses may be cached temporarily for performance
- Historical quiz data is stored locally on your device
- You can clear this data anytime from your browser/app settings

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### With Your Consent
We may share information with your explicit consent.

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

1. **Google Gemini AI** - For generating quiz questions and AI tutor responses
   - We send your topics and questions to Google's AI service
   - Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
   - Link: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. **Google Sign-In** - For authentication (if you choose to use it)
   - We receive your basic profile information (name, email, photo)
   - Governed by Google's OAuth Privacy Policy

3. **Cloudflare** (Optional fallback AI provider)
   - Used only if primary AI service is unavailable
   - Governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
